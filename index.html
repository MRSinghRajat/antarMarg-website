What is the meaning of dharma in Bhagavad Gita?
🙏 Namaste! In the Bhagavad Gita, dharma (धर्म) refers to one's sacred duty, righteousness, and the moral law that sustains cosmic order. Krishna teaches Arjuna that fulfilling one's svadharma — personal duty — is the highest path, even if imperfectly done. "श्रेयान्स्वधर्मो विगुणः" — Better is one's own dharma, though imperfect. (Ch. 3, Verse 35)
Which mantra should I chant for peace of mind?
For inner peace, I recommend the Shanti Mantra:

ॐ सर्वेशां स्वस्तिर्भवतु

Chant it 108 times daily during Brahma Muhurta (4:30-6:00 AM) for best results. 🙏
